Content Strategy

I began my creative career as a writer before becoming a UX designer. In the last 10 years, I’ve written site copy, interface copy, social media posts, video scripts, investigative articles, service articles, blogs, press releases and internal newsletters.


Mic News

Reporting | Headline Writing | Social Media
Mic is a leading digital news company. During my two-year tenure there, the site attracted over 66 million unique readers and viewers each month. As a journalist, being able to tell a compelling story was tantamount my my success. The UX principles involved in my work included:
  • Researching and assessing the needs of of Mic’s readership to determine opportunities for food and travel-related content
  • Thoughtfully crafting headlines, selecting media, and developing social media copy to increase the click-rate and encourage social sharing
  • Collaborating with visual designers to create original illustrations for editorial packages
  • Locating, recruiting and interviewing relevant sources quickly

DailyBurn

Service Journalism | SEO | UX Writing
Life by DailyBurn is a health and fitness company that provides online workout and nutrition programs. As a member of the small team who launched the editorial website in 2013, I helped our readership grow to over three million monthly unique readers. I used the following UX skills:
  • Collaborating with engineers to implement Pinterest-friendly metadata into our Wordpress site
  • Leveraging Google Analytics to find opportunities for SEO-driven content
  • Developing and editing content for DailyBurn’s recipe finder interface
  • Understanding the health and fitness-related needs and pain points of different demographics; creating fitness and nutrition editorial content to address those needs
  • Creating and embedding visuals (slideshows, photo collages and GIFs) to enhance a reader’s experience
