I'm a curious UX designer & journalist.


Asking good questions is my superpower.

I uncover actionable insights that inform smart UX design decisions. I am a systems thinker who likes to consider how interconnected parts can become more than their sum. I specialize in scoping projects, investigating problems, researching opportunities, and coordinating cross-functional teams towards a unified objective.

I’m passionate about health tech, food supply chains and education. Working as a journalist covering fitness, nutrition and supply chain transparency initially sparked my interest in these topics. I’m fascinated by how technology in these sectors has the potential to shape and enhance our lives.

I strive to empower those around me and include them in the design process. Collaborating with other people with different skill sets gives me energy.
