process

  • 1. Understand People + Problem Space

    The team immerses themselves in a domain to understand competitors, technical limitations, and team hypotheses and assumptions. We collect data on end-users.

  • 2. Define the Opportunity and the Plan

    Through data synthesis, we uncover actionable insights. With teammates and stakeholders, we define the opportunity area and share insights about the end-user. We make a time-bound plan for action and set KPIs.

  • 3. Create and Design Solutions

    We dive into design. Ideas and opportunities are transformed into something tangible. Internal teams iterate and improve upon designs before usability testing begins.

  • 4. Measure Success + Iterate Designs

    Thoroughly testing designs and prototypes with the appropriate users is essential. We analyze how successful the UX and UI are, and iterate accordingly.

  • 5. Deliver Solutions

    After validating designs, we increase fidelity and prepare for development.

    Again!
