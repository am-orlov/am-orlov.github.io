Content Strategy

I began my creative career reporting for editorial websites before becoming a UX designer. In the last 10 years, I’ve written site copy, interface copy, social media posts, video scripts, investigative articles, service articles, blogs, press releases and internal newsletters.




Mic News

Reporting | Headline Writing | Social Media
Mic is a news website that covers progressive issues and social justice. Working as journalist and assistant editor at Mic for two years, I wrote and edited over 500 news and feature articles. These timely articles were engineered to spark conversations among Mic’s loyal readership. Each headline and piece of artwork was carefully chosen to entice clicks and social shares. Meanwhile the content was concisely written to deliver news to readers with short attention spans. As a reporter, I excelled at locating and interviewing relevant sources quickly in order to meet deadlines.

Life by DailyBurn

Service Journalism | SEO | UX Writing
Life by DailyBurn is an editorial website that provides health and wellness articles, videos and guides. I was a key member of the small team that launched the editorial website in 2013. To grow our readership from zero to over three million monthly unique readers, the team embedded within the health and fitness online community and crafted a unique and approachable editorial voice. We used Google Analytics to find opportunities for us to develop SEO-driven fitness content. Additionally, I helped the product team develop and adapt content for DailyBurn’s recipe finder interface.
